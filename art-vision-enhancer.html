Art Vision Enhancer Chatbot

Cambridge Research Project

Project Overview

The aim of this project was to develop a chatbot capable of engaging with students on their art concepts, encouraging deeper exploration and creativity through intelligent questioning and dialogue.

Deliverables

The Art Vision Enhancer Chatbot is designed to interact with students by understanding their descriptions of art concepts and prompting them to explore their ideas further. Leveraging advanced natural language processing (NLP) techniques, the chatbot interprets student responses and generates follow-up questions that simulate a creative brainstorming session. This adaptive questioning mechanism enhances the learning experience, encouraging students to think critically and expand on their initial ideas.

Technologies Used

Programming Language: Python
Framework: Django
API: ChatGPT-4 API for natural language understanding and dialogue generation
NLP Techniques: Used for parsing and interpreting student responses
Machine Learning: Applied to develop the chatbot's adaptive questioning mechanism
